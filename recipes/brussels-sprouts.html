Bacon and Honey Roasted Brussels Sprouts

Description

Brussels sprouts are roasted with honey and bacon till crispy and caramelised. They are easy, fast and too delicious for words!

Ingredients

  • 2lbs of brussels sprouts
  • 1/2-1lb of bacon
  • 2-3 springs thyme
  • 1 tablespoon olive
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 tablespoon cider or balsamic vinegar
  • And salt and pepper!

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to400F/200C. Cut the sprouts in half and remove the hard stems at the bottom as well as the outer leaves if damaged.
  2. On a large rimmed baking sheet toss together the sprouts, olive oil, salt and pepper, thyme leaves and chopped bacon.
  3. Place the brussels sprouts on the baking sheet cut side down for maximum caramelisation.
  4. Roast in the oven for 15 minutes.
  5. While that roasts, mix the honey and vinegar in small bowl.
  6. After 15 minutes take out the brussels and drizzle with the honey and vinegar mix and stir to coat evenly.
  7. Put them back in the oven for 10 more minutes.
  8. Broil for 1 minute to get it crispy and for some extra browning.
