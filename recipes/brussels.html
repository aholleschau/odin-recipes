Oven Roasted Brussels Sprouts

Description

What was once an often despised vegetable, these oven roasted brussels sprouts are reimagined with beautiful, subtle seasoning and lightly roasted for a delicious maillard browning sure to please even the most skeptical and particular eaters.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs fresh, whole brussels sprouts
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp fresh ground black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp local honey
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
